Privacy Policy – Password Generator

Data collection

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

App usage

All passwords are generated locally on the device in real time as the user adjust any option. No data is transmitted to external servers or is stored or retained locally.

Advertising and tracking

The app does not display ads and does not use tracking services.

Permissions

The app only requests minimal permissions required for its core functionality (e.g., clipboard access to copy passwords).

Changes

If this policy is updated, the latest version will be published at this same link.
